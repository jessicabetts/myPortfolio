About the Anchients

For those who survived the Fall of Atlantis the world has become a dark and dreaded place. Where once there was a way to save the those souls who's bodies have failed them, now there is only the way of Daveth, the way of Death. Only nine survived the explosion of the source, absorbing the blue energy into themselves, and of those nine only Avaris Mattea has the power to bring back the souls of the dead. The only question is, how much pain and heartache and death will it take until she finds the power within herself to rip through the very fabric of life itself?

Below are the stories of the Nine, the ones who survived:

  • Avaris Mattea, Goddess of Necromancy

  • Daveth Mattea, Creator of Death.

  • Judas Lux, Guide to the Afterlife.

  • Ian Kame, Spirit of Chaos

  • Charlot Blake, Mother of Witchcraft.
