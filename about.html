About the Anchients

For those who survived the Fall of Atlantis the world had become a dark and dreaded place. Where once there were ways to save the souls who's bodies failed them, now there is only the way of Daveth. Only nine survived the explosion, absorbing the energy of the source, and of those nine only Avaris Mattea has the power to bring back the souls of the dead. The only question is, how much pain and heartache and death will it take until she finds the power within herself to rip through the very fabric of life itself?
